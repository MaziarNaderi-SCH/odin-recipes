Potato Salad

Description:

This red potato salad is a creamy, tangy side dish packed with flavor from ranch dressing and herbs. Itâ€™s great for picnics and barbecues.

Ingredients:

  • 6 red potatoes, cut into chunks
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 2 tablespoons chopped chives
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil potatoes until tender, then drain and let cool.
  2. In a large bowl, mix potatoes with ranch dressing, chives, and garlic powder.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Refrigerate before serving.
