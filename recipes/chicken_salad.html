Chicken Salad

Description:

This creamy chicken salad is quick, delicious, and perfect for sandwiches or lettuce wraps. It combines tender chicken with crunchy celery and a smooth dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken, celery, and lemon juice.
  2. Add mayonnaise and mix until well combined.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Chill before serving for best flavor.
