Cheesy Bacon Ranch Sliders Burger

Description:

These mini bacon ranch sliders are loaded with flavor. Juicy beef, melted cheese, and crispy bacon all packed into bite-sized buns â€” perfect for parties and gatherings.

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 6 strips cooked bacon, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ranch dressing
  • Mini slider buns
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Mix beef with salt, pepper, and ranch dressing.
  2. Form into small patties and cook until browned.
  3. Top with cheese and let it melt.
  4. Place on slider buns with bacon bits.
